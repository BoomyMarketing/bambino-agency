Our Story

Born in Manchester, Built for British Business

Bambino was founded in 2019 by Sophie H., who left a senior position at a Manchester media agency after growing frustrated with an industry that prioritised retainer fees over real results. She had watched too many UK businesses pour money into digital marketing campaigns that looked impressive in monthly reports but failed to move the needle on revenue, leads, or growth.

Her founding belief was simple: British businesses deserve marketing that actually works. Transparent strategies. Honest reporting. Real, measurable outcomes. No jargon, no hidden fees, and no long-term contracts designed to lock clients in rather than deliver results.

Starting with just five local Manchester clients in early 2019, Bambino grew rapidly — not through aggressive sales tactics, but through referrals from clients who were seeing results they hadn’t experienced with previous agencies. By the end of our first year, we had grown to 30 clients. By 2022, we crossed 200. Today, we support over 400 businesses across the UK, from independent retailers and professional service firms in the North West to venture-backed SaaS companies in London and Bristol.

Our team has grown alongside our client base. What started as a four-person operation now includes specialist practitioners across SEO, paid media, web development, AI automation, content strategy, and more — all working from Manchester, delivering UK-wide results.

How We Work

Our 4-Step Growth Methodology

Every Bambino engagement follows the same proven framework — built on 7 years of refining what works for UK businesses.

1

Audit

We conduct a comprehensive audit of your current digital presence — analytics, competitors, technical health, and growth opportunities — before recommending anything.

2

Strategy

We build a bespoke growth strategy tailored to your specific goals, industry, budget, and competitive landscape. No templates. No generic plans.

3

Execute

Our specialists execute the strategy across every agreed channel — SEO, paid media, web, content, or AI — with full transparency on what’s being done and why.

4

Report & Refine

Monthly plain-English reports show exactly what’s working, what isn’t, and what we’re doing next. We continuously refine based on data, not assumptions.
