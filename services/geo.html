What We Do

The New Frontier of Search Visibility

Search behaviour is shifting faster than ever. A growing proportion of UK consumers now turn to AI tools — ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and Bing Copilot — to answer their questions and discover businesses. When someone asks "what's the best digital marketing agency in Manchester?" or "who does PPC for UK e-commerce?", will your brand appear in the answer?

Generative Engine Optimisation (GEO) is the discipline of making your content, your brand signals, and your website structure irresistible to AI language models as a citation source. It builds on strong SEO foundations whilst adding a new layer of AI-specific optimisation — passage-level citability, brand authority signals, structured data depth, and llms.txt implementation — to ensure AI engines choose your brand over your competitors.

Common Questions

GEO Questions Answered

GEO is the practice of optimising your content and digital presence so that AI-powered search engines — such as ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and Bing Copilot — cite your brand in their responses. As more people use AI to answer questions rather than clicking through traditional search results, appearing in AI-generated answers is becoming as important as ranking on Google page one.

Traditional SEO focuses on ranking in Google's 10 blue links. GEO focuses on being selected as a trustworthy, citable source by AI systems that synthesise answers from across the web. Both disciplines overlap significantly — strong E-E-A-T, authoritative content, and structured data help with both — but GEO additionally requires passage-level citability, brand mention signals, and llms.txt implementation.

We optimise for Google AI Overviews (formerly SGE), ChatGPT with web search, Perplexity, Bing Copilot, and Gemini Advanced. The underlying signals that lead to citations on these platforms are largely consistent, so improvements tend to lift visibility across all AI search tools simultaneously.

Initial citation improvements can appear within 6–12 weeks for less competitive queries as AI systems re-index and reassess sources. Building consistent brand citation authority across competitive topics typically takes 3–6 months of sustained GEO activity. We track citation frequency, brand mention volume, and AI Overview appearance rate monthly.
