How It Works

Our 4-Step Process

A clear, proven methodology — from needs assessment to a team that uses AI every day.

1

Assess & Design

We assess your team’s AI maturity, current tool stack, and biggest time sinks — then design a bespoke workshop agenda using your brand, content, and channels as the raw material for every exercise.

2

Deliver Workshop

We run a hands-on full-day or half-day workshop where every attendee actively uses AI tools on real tasks — not passive demonstrations. Groups are kept small (max 12) to ensure everyone participates fully.

3

Embed & Practise

Each attendee receives a personal AI toolkit document with their practised prompts and workflows. We set 30-day implementation goals so the team applies learning immediately rather than returning to old habits.

4

Follow Up & Measure

We check in at 30 and 60 days post-workshop to answer questions, address adoption blockers, and measure the impact on content output, publishing speed, and channel performance against pre-training baselines.
