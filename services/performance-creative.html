What We Do

What Is Performance Creative?

Performance creative is advertising creative — static images, video, carousels, GIFs — specifically designed and continuously optimised for paid media performance. Unlike brand creative, which focuses on aesthetics and long-term identity, performance creative is built around hard conversion metrics: thumb-stop ratio, click-through rate, cost per acquisition, and return on ad spend.

Every element of a performance creative — the hook, the headline, the visual hierarchy, the call to action — is a testable hypothesis. Our team produces creative across Meta, TikTok, YouTube, and Google Display using data-driven briefs, then iterates each variant based on platform signals. Creative that performs is scaled; creative that underperforms is replaced with better-informed iterations. The result is a compound improvement in creative performance over time, and a significant reduction in wasted ad spend.

Key insight: Nielsen research shows creative quality drives 56% of ad ROI — more than targeting, media placement, and brand combined. Underinvesting in creative is the most common cause of declining ROAS for UK paid media accounts.

Creative Testing

Creative Testing Methodology

A structured A/B creative testing framework that turns every campaign into a learning engine.

Hook Testing

The first 1–3 seconds of any ad determine whether a user stops scrolling. We systematically test multiple hooks — questions, bold claims, social proof openers, and visual surprises — to identify which narrative entry points drive the highest thumb-stop ratio in your specific audience.

Format Testing

Different audiences respond to different formats. We test static vs video, short-form vs long-form, UGC-style vs polished brand, and single image vs carousel — using platform data to determine the optimal format mix for each campaign objective and audience segment.

Audience × Creative Matrix

We build creative matrices that pair each audience segment with tailored creative angles — ensuring the right message and visual approach reaches the right person. Broad audiences often respond to different hooks than retargeting audiences or lookalikes, so we test all combinations.

Winning Creative Scaling

When a creative variant demonstrates clear outperformance — defined as exceeding account-average CPA or ROAS by 20% or more across sufficient spend — we scale it through budget increases, broader audiences, and cross-platform adaptation. Winning creative is never wasted.

Is It Right for You?

When to Use Performance Creative

Performance creative delivers the biggest impact in four specific scenarios. If any of these sound familiar, it’s time to invest in creative.

Scaling to £10K+ per Month in Ad Spend

At higher spend levels, creative quality becomes the primary lever for maintaining ROAS. A single winning creative variant can sustain efficient scaling for weeks — but only if you have the volume to find it. Our programme generates enough variants to keep your testing pipeline full as you scale.

Experiencing Creative Fatigue

If your cost per acquisition is rising while your targeting and bidding haven’t changed, creative fatigue is the most likely cause. Audiences tire of seeing the same ads after repeated exposure — fresh creative is the fastest fix. We produce new variants monthly to keep fatigue at bay.

Declining ROAS Despite Stable Strategy

Declining ROAS with no changes to budgets or targeting almost always points to underperforming creative. Rather than guessing which ads to fix, our testing methodology quickly identifies what’s working and what isn’t — and replaces weak creative with data-informed alternatives.

Launching on a New Platform

Each paid media platform has its own creative culture, format requirements, and audience expectations. When you expand to a new channel — TikTok, YouTube, or programmatic display — repurposing existing creative rarely works. We build platform-native creative from the ground up.
