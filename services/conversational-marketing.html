What We Do

What Is Conversational Marketing?

Conversational marketing replaces the passive, one-way lead capture form with real-time, two-way dialogue. Using chatbots, live chat, WhatsApp Business, Instagram DM automation, and AI messaging assistants, it creates a personalised 1:1 experience at scale — engaging every visitor at the exact moment of interest rather than asking them to complete a form and wait for someone to ring back.

The shift matters commercially: 82% of consumers say an immediate response is important when they have a question, but the average response time for UK businesses using contact forms alone is over 12 hours. Conversational marketing closes that gap entirely. By qualifying leads in real time, routing them to the right salesperson or booking them directly into a meeting, and handling common objections through carefully designed conversation flows, it consistently generates 3–5x more qualified leads from the same volume of traffic — without increasing ad spend.

Website Chatbot

A proactive chatbot on your website greets visitors based on page, time on site, and referral source — qualifying intent, answering FAQs, and routing hot leads directly to your sales team or a calendar booking link.

Automated and semi-automated LinkedIn outreach sequences for B2B sales development, event promotion, and partnership development — with personalisation at scale.

Two-way SMS enables real-time qualification, appointment booking confirmation, and re-engagement conversations with leads who have opted in — with response rates far exceeding email.

AI vs Rule-Based

AI vs Rule-Based Chatbots: Which Is Right for You?

Both have advantages. For most UK businesses, a hybrid approach delivers the best results — using AI where flexibility matters and rule-based flows where precision is critical.

Feature AI Chatbot Rule-Based Chatbot Hybrid (Our Recommendation)
Language Handling Natural language, handles unexpected phrasing and questions Exact keywords and button clicks only; breaks on unexpected input AI for open-ended questions, rule-based for structured qualification steps
Predictability Can produce unexpected responses; requires guardrails and monitoring Fully predictable; every response is defined and controlled Predictable for high-stakes flows; flexible for exploratory conversations
Setup Complexity Higher — requires training data, knowledge base, and ongoing tuning Lower — faster to build for defined use cases with limited scope Moderate — rule-based structure with AI layer for unscripted moments
Best For Support desks, knowledge bases, exploratory Q&A across large product ranges Lead qualification, appointment booking, structured sales flows Most business use cases where both conversion precision and user flexibility matter

Common Questions

Conversational Marketing FAQs

Conversational marketing is the practice of using real-time, two-way dialogue — via chatbots, live chat, WhatsApp Business, AI assistants, and messaging platforms — to engage website visitors and prospects at the moment they are most interested, rather than asking them to fill in a passive form and wait for a callback. By replacing static lead capture with dynamic conversation, businesses qualify leads faster, reduce friction in the sales process, and create a 1:1 experience at scale.

A chatbot is one tool within conversational marketing, not conversational marketing itself. Conversational marketing is the broader strategy: designing the right questions, routing logic, and response sequences to move a prospect from initial curiosity to a qualified conversation with your team. Chatbots automate parts of that journey, but conversational marketing also includes live chat escalation, WhatsApp Business flows, Instagram DM automation, SMS two-way messaging, and the conversation design that makes all of these feel natural rather than robotic.

Yes — B2B is one of the strongest use cases for conversational marketing. Long B2B sales cycles mean prospects visit your website multiple times before converting; a well-designed chatbot or live chat experience captures intent in real time, qualifies the lead (company size, budget, timeline, pain point), and routes them to the right salesperson or books a meeting directly. Our B2B conversational marketing clients see an average 3.5x improvement in lead-to-meeting conversion rates compared to contact forms alone.

Platform recommendation depends on your use case and existing tech stack. For B2B SaaS and professional services, Intercom and Drift (now Salesloft) are the most powerful, with strong CRM integrations and AI capabilities. HubSpot Conversations is ideal if you are already on the HubSpot CRM ecosystem. For ecommerce and D2C brands needing Facebook Messenger and Instagram automation, ManyChat is the leading platform. For businesses wanting a simple, affordable live chat and chatbot solution, Tidio is excellent. For WhatsApp Business at scale, the official WhatsApp Business Platform (via Meta) or a BSP like Respond.io is required. We recommend based on your specific requirements.

We measure conversational marketing ROI across five dimensions: Conversation Volume (how many unique conversations the system handles monthly), Qualification Rate (percentage of conversations that produce a qualified lead or booking), Lead-to-Close Rate from conversational leads vs contact form leads, Response Time (average time from visitor intent to first meaningful response), and Revenue Attributed (deals won that originated from a conversational marketing touchpoint). We build a reporting dashboard that tracks all five metrics monthly alongside cost per qualified lead.

Yes — CRM integration is a standard part of every conversational marketing implementation we deliver. We connect your chatbot or live chat platform to your CRM (HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, Zoho, or others) so that every qualified conversation automatically creates or updates a contact record, logs the conversation transcript, assigns the lead to the right salesperson, and triggers any relevant follow-up sequences. This eliminates manual data entry and ensures no lead falls through the cracks between your conversational channel and your sales pipeline.
