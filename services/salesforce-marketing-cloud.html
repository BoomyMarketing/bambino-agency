What Is SFMC?

Salesforce Marketing Cloud, Explained

Salesforce Marketing Cloud (SFMC) is the world’s leading enterprise marketing automation platform, built for organisations that need to manage complex, data-driven customer journeys at scale. Unlike mid-market tools, SFMC is designed around relational data models, allowing marketers to draw on multiple data sources — purchase history, CRM records, web behaviour, and third-party data — to deliver highly personalised communications across email, SMS, push notifications, social advertising, and the web.

SFMC is not a single tool but a suite of integrated studios and builders. Each studio serves a specific channel or function — Email Studio for bulk email, Journey Builder for automated multi-step customer journeys, Mobile Studio for SMS and push, Advertising Studio for social and display retargeting, Interaction Studio (now called Marketing Cloud Personalisation) for real-time web personalisation, and Analytics Builder for cross-channel reporting.

For UK enterprise organisations already using Salesforce CRM (Sales Cloud or Service Cloud), SFMC is the natural choice: it connects directly via Marketing Cloud Connect, enabling marketers to trigger journeys from CRM events, sync contact data in real time, and close the loop between marketing and sales activity.

SFMC Studios We Implement

Salesforce Marketing Cloud is not a single tool — it’s a suite of purpose-built studios. We implement and integrate all six core studios for complete cross-channel coverage.

Email Studio

The core of SFMC’s sending capability. We design, build, and manage segmented email campaigns using AMPscript personalisation, dynamic content blocks, and A/B testing to maximise open rates, click rates, and revenue per email.

Journey Builder

SFMC’s multi-step automation canvas. We architect welcome series, re-engagement journeys, post-purchase sequences, and complex event-triggered workflows that respond to customer behaviour across every touchpoint.

Advertising Studio

Connect your SFMC contact data to paid advertising on Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Google, and YouTube. Suppress existing customers from acquisition campaigns, and retarget lapsed contacts with precision using first-party data.

Mobile Studio

SMS, push notifications, and in-app messaging managed directly within SFMC. Ideal for time-sensitive communications, appointment reminders, delivery updates, and promotional alerts that require high open rates and immediate action.

Interaction Studio / Personalisation

Real-time web and email personalisation powered by machine learning. Serve personalised content, product recommendations, and journey paths based on individual browsing behaviour, purchase history, and predictive engagement scores.

Analytics Builder

Cross-channel reporting and business intelligence built into SFMC. We configure custom dashboards covering email performance, journey completion rates, channel attribution, subscriber engagement scores, and revenue impact metrics.

Compliance

UK GDPR & SFMC Compliance

SFMC provides powerful compliance tooling, but it must be configured correctly to meet UK GDPR obligations. We ensure your SFMC instance is compliant from day one, with the systems to maintain compliance as your marketing activity scales.

Data Residency

We configure your SFMC instance to use EU/UK data centre locations where available, document your data flows for ROPA (Record of Processing Activities) purposes, and advise on data transfer mechanisms compliant with the UK GDPR and Adequacy Decisions.

Consent Management

We implement a consent data model within your SFMC data extensions, capturing the legal basis for each communication, the source of consent, and the consent date — ensuring you can demonstrate lawful processing for every email sent.

Suppression Lists

We configure global and publication-specific suppression lists to prevent sending to opted-out, bounced, or legally suppressed contacts, and set up automated processes to keep suppression lists current when consent is withdrawn.

Preference Centre

We build a branded SFMC-hosted preference centre allowing subscribers to manage their communication preferences at category level — compliant with PECR (Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations) and ICO guidance on email marketing.

Common Questions

Salesforce Marketing Cloud FAQs

Salesforce Marketing Cloud (SFMC) is the world’s leading enterprise marketing automation platform, built for large organisations managing complex, multi-channel customer journeys. It comprises a suite of studios and builders — Email Studio, Journey Builder, Advertising Studio, Mobile Studio, Interaction Studio and Analytics Builder — that together enable personalised marketing at scale. SFMC is particularly powerful when integrated with Salesforce CRM, allowing seamless data flow between sales and marketing teams.

Salesforce CRM (Sales Cloud) manages your sales pipeline, contacts, and opportunities. Salesforce Marketing Cloud is a separate platform focused entirely on marketing execution — email campaigns, customer journeys, mobile messaging, advertising, and marketing analytics. The two platforms integrate via Marketing Cloud Connect, enabling marketers to access CRM contact data and trigger journeys based on sales pipeline events.

SFMC is the right choice when you have a database of 150,000+ contacts, operate multiple brands or business units, require advanced data modelling with relational data extensions, need deep integration with Salesforce CRM Sales Cloud or Service Cloud, or require enterprise-grade compliance and data residency controls. For organisations with simpler needs, fewer contacts, or tighter budgets, HubSpot Marketing Hub typically offers better value and faster implementation.
