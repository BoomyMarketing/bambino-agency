What We Do

Backlinks That Actually Move Rankings

Backlinks remain one of Google's top three ranking factors — and they're one of the hardest signals to fake. A strong backlink profile tells Google that real, authoritative sources trust your content enough to link to it. That trust translates directly into higher rankings, more organic traffic, and compounding domain authority that protects your position over time.

We earn editorial links from genuine UK publications, industry media, and authoritative resource pages — not link farms, not PBNs, not networks of low-quality sites. Every placement is transparent, every anchor text is deliberate, and every link we build is one we'd be happy to show Google directly.

Link building benchmarks (Bambino, 2025): Clients who consistently add 4–8 new DR 40–65 referring domains per month see an average 38% organic traffic increase within 6 months. Our average link placement has a DR of 52, with 94% placed on real editorial sites with genuine organic traffic (verified via Ahrefs).

How It Works

Our 4-Step Link Building Process

A transparent, methodical approach to earning links that actually move your rankings — no shortcuts, no guesswork.

1

Analyse

We audit your current backlink profile, map competitor link gaps, identify your highest-value target pages, and build a prioritised list of link opportunities specific to your niche and domain authority.

2

Create

We develop linkable assets and Digital PR angles tailored to your industry — original data, expert commentary, research-led content, and resource pages that UK journalists and editors want to cite.

3

Outreach

Our team contacts UK journalists, editors, webmasters, and resource curators with personalised, relevant pitches. We leverage established relationships with UK publications to place links efficiently and at scale.

4

Report

Every new link is documented in your monthly report: the live URL, referring domain, Domain Rating, estimated traffic, anchor text used, and target page. Full transparency, every month.
