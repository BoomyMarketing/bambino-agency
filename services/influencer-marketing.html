What We Do

What Is Influencer Marketing?

Influencer marketing is the practice of partnering with individuals who have built trust and influence with a specific audience to promote your brand, product, or service. Unlike traditional advertising, influencer content reaches consumers in their natural digital environment — on the feed, in Stories, on YouTube — delivered by a voice they already follow and trust.

In the UK, influencer marketing has matured significantly. Audiences are more discerning, the ASA enforces disclosure rules rigorously, and brands increasingly demand campaign data beyond vanity metrics. The most effective UK influencer campaigns in 2026 are built on precise creator matching, clear contractual obligations, genuine creative collaboration, and hard conversion data — not just reach and impressions.

Whether your objective is brand awareness, product launch amplification, ongoing content production, or affiliate-style performance marketing, Bambino builds an influencer strategy mapped to your specific goals, audience, and budget. We manage every element in-house: creator sourcing and vetting, contracting, briefing, content approval, publishing, and reporting.

UK Legal Compliance

ASA Compliance & UK Advertising Law

UK influencer marketing is regulated by the Advertising Standards Authority (ASA) under the CAP Code. Non-compliant campaigns can result in formal ASA rulings, reputational damage for your brand, and creators being removed from platforms. We ensure every campaign we manage is fully compliant — because the legal risk sits with the brand, not just the creator.

Our compliance process is built into every contract and content review, so you never have to worry about a campaign becoming a headline for the wrong reasons.

Ask About Our Compliance Process →
#ad
#ad or #advert — at the start, every time

The ASA requires that paid posts are labelled with #ad or #advert placed prominently at the beginning of the caption — not buried in hashtags, not at the end, not in a small overlay. We enforce this contractually and review all posts before publication.

CAP
CAP Code compliance

The UK CAP Code governs what claims influencers can make about your product. Health claims, comparative claims, and price claims all have specific rules. We brief creators on content restrictions and review all claims before approval.

GIFT
Gifted product disclosure

Gifted products — even when no payment is made — must be disclosed with #gifted or #gift. This applies to PR send-outs and product loans. We advise clients on gifting vs. paid partnership classification and ensure disclosure is correct in all cases.

AFFL
Affiliate & commission disclosure

Where creators earn commission from sales (affiliate arrangements), this must also be disclosed. We structure affiliate influencer agreements with compliant disclosure language and ensure tracking links are implemented correctly.

Campaign Formats

Influencer Campaign Types

We run four distinct campaign structures, each designed for a specific marketing objective. Many campaigns combine two or more types for compounding results.

Awareness Campaigns

Designed to maximise reach and brand recognition with a new or existing audience. Typically uses a mix of macro and mega creators to generate broad exposure in a short window — most effective for product launches, seasonal moments, or entering a new market segment.

  • KPIs: reach, impressions, CPM, brand search uplift
  • Best tier: macro + mega
  • Timeline: 2–4 weeks
  • Platforms: Instagram, TikTok, YouTube

Product Launch Campaigns

A phased influencer strategy timed to a product release: teaser content from select creators ahead of launch, hero content on launch day, and follow-up review content in the weeks that follow. Combines multiple tiers for both breadth and credibility.

  • KPIs: reach, engagement, website traffic, sales
  • Best tier: micro + macro mix
  • Timeline: 4–8 weeks (including pre-launch seeding)
  • Platforms: Instagram, TikTok, YouTube

Affiliate & Performance Campaigns

Creator partnerships structured around measurable commercial outcomes — promo codes, UTM-tracked links, and commission on sales. Ideal for ecommerce brands and subscription products where every pound of spend must be tracked to a return. Often paired with Meta Ads whitelisting for paid amplification.

  • KPIs: clicks, conversions, CPA, ROAS
  • Best tier: nano + micro
  • Timeline: ongoing monthly
  • Platforms: Instagram, TikTok, YouTube

Always-On Programmes

A retained roster of creators producing regular content for your brand on a monthly or quarterly basis. Provides consistent social proof, a steady stream of authentic UGC for your own channels, and compounding brand-building effects. Works particularly well alongside our Social Media Marketing and UGC Content Production services.

  • KPIs: content volume, engagement rate, share of voice
  • Best tier: nano + micro
  • Timeline: 3–12 month retainer
  • Platforms: brand-dependent
