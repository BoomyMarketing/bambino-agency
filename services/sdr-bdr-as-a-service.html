What Is It?

Outsourced Sales Development — Human-Led, Not Automated

SDR and BDR as a Service means you get dedicated sales development professionals working on behalf of your business — without the cost, time, and risk of recruiting them yourself. Our reps embed directly into your team, use your tools, and represent your brand in every conversation.

An SDR (Sales Development Representative) focuses on qualifying inbound leads: engaging prospects who have shown interest, asking discovery questions, and passing sales-ready opportunities to your account executives. A BDR (Business Development Representative) works the other direction — pure outbound prospecting, identifying and approaching companies that fit your ideal customer profile and have never heard of you.

Both roles are human-in-the-loop: real people making real judgements, building genuine rapport, and adapting messaging in real time. This is fundamentally different from automated outbound sequences. Where automation sends volume, our SDRs and BDRs build pipeline.

UK Compliance

Fully Compliant UK Sales Outreach

Sales development in the UK operates within a clear regulatory framework. We follow all applicable rules as a matter of standard practice — not an afterthought.

Cold calling — TPS & CTPS: Under the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR), businesses must not make unsolicited marketing calls to numbers registered with the Telephone Preference Service (TPS) or, for businesses, the Corporate Telephone Preference Service (CTPS). Every dial list we build is screened against both registers before any calls are made.

Email outreach — UK GDPR & PECR: B2B email outreach to corporate addresses is permissible under UK GDPR’s legitimate interests basis, provided it is relevant, proportionate, and includes a clear opt-out mechanism. We follow ICO guidance on B2B email and include unsubscribe links on every communication. We do not purchase or use data from non-compliant sources.

LinkedIn outreach — fair use: Our reps use LinkedIn Sales Navigator within the platform’s terms of service. Connection requests and messages are sent manually, at human rates, and never via scraping tools or automated bots that violate LinkedIn’s user agreement.
