Why HubSpot?

The CRM That Unifies Your Entire Revenue Operation

Most UK businesses manage their marketing, sales, and customer service across a patchwork of disconnected tools — a CRM that doesn’t talk to the email platform, a sales tool that can’t see marketing engagement, a support desk with no CRM context. The result is missed follow-ups, duplicated effort, and revenue that slips through the gaps between teams.

HubSpot solves this by placing your entire revenue operation on a single, unified platform. Every contact record shows complete lifecycle history: the emails they opened, the pages they visited, the deals they’re in, and the support tickets they’ve raised. Marketing campaigns trigger sales sequences. Closed deals automatically update customer records. Renewals and upsells are flagged proactively based on customer health scores.

For UK businesses generating between £500K and £50M in annual revenue, HubSpot Professional or Enterprise represents the most cost-effective path to a fully integrated revenue tech stack — provided it’s implemented correctly from the start. That’s where Bambino comes in.

The 5 HubSpot Hubs

Bambino implements and manages all five HubSpot Hubs, individually or as a fully integrated platform. Each Hub delivers stand-alone value — together they create a revenue machine.

Marketing Hub

Email marketing, landing pages, forms, blog, SEO recommendations, social publishing, ads management, and marketing automation workflows. The Marketing Hub connects campaign activity directly to contact records and pipeline revenue, giving you true attribution across every channel.

Sales Hub

Deal pipelines, contact sequences, meeting scheduling, call recording, sales forecasting, and document tracking. Sales Hub gives your team complete visibility of every prospect relationship, automates routine follow-up tasks, and surfaces insights that help close deals faster.

Service Hub

Shared inbox, ticketing, live chat, knowledge base, customer feedback surveys (NPS, CSAT, CES), and customer portal. Service Hub connects support activity to CRM data so your team can resolve issues faster and proactively identify accounts at risk of churn.

CMS Hub

A developer-friendly CMS with native CRM integration, smart content personalisation, built-in SEO recommendations, and A/B testing. CMS Hub lets you create personalised website experiences for known contacts — showing different content to a lead vs a customer vs a churned account.

Operations Hub

Data sync, workflow automation, programmable bots, data quality automation, and custom reporting. Operations Hub is the connective tissue of HubSpot — keeping your CRM data clean, your integrations in sync, and your automated processes running reliably across all Hubs.

Why All 5?

Each Hub delivers immediate value independently. But the real power of HubSpot emerges when all Hubs share the same contact and company data — marketing triggers sales actions, service history informs renewal strategy, and Operations Hub keeps everything clean and synced. Bambino architects your portal for long-term cross-Hub leverage from day one.

Compliance

HubSpot & UK GDPR Compliance

Every HubSpot portal we implement is configured to meet UK GDPR and PECR requirements. Compliance isn’t an afterthought — it’s built into the architecture from day one.

GDPR-Compliant Consent Forms

We configure HubSpot forms with explicit opt-in checkboxes that record the lawful basis and consent text at the point of capture. Consent data is stored on the contact record and is fully auditable, with timestamps, IP addresses, and the exact consent language used.

Data Retention Policies

We set up automated data retention workflows that flag contacts who haven’t engaged within your defined retention period, trigger re-consent campaigns before deletion, and archive or purge records in line with your data retention schedule and ICO requirements.

PECR for Marketing Emails

HubSpot’s subscription management system is configured to enforce PECR-compliant email marketing — ensuring contacts can only receive marketing communications they’ve specifically opted into, and that unsubscribe requests are honoured immediately across all communication types.

Lawful Basis Configuration

We configure HubSpot’s built-in lawful basis tracking for each contact type, mapping the correct legal basis (consent, legitimate interest, contract, etc.) to each data processing activity. This ensures your portal can demonstrate compliance during an ICO audit or data subject access request.
