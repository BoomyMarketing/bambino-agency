What We Do

What Is a Fractional CMO?

A fractional CMO (Chief Marketing Officer) is a senior marketing executive who works with your business on a part-time, shared basis — typically 2–4 days per week — rather than as a full-time employee. They bring the same strategic seniority, board-level credibility, and functional depth as a full-time CMO, but at a fraction of the cost.

Your fractional CMO owns the marketing strategy end to end: setting direction, managing your internal team, overseeing your agency relationships, controlling the marketing budget, and reporting performance to the CEO and board. They are accountable for outcomes — not just outputs. Unlike a marketing consultant who advises and leaves, or an agency that executes without owning the strategy, a fractional CMO is a genuine member of your leadership team.

The fractional model has become the default choice for UK businesses that need executive marketing leadership but are not yet at the scale — or do not yet have the revenue certainty — to justify a £150K–£250K salary package. It is particularly popular with Series A and B start-ups, private equity-backed SMEs, founder-led businesses scaling beyond £2M ARR, and businesses bridging a gap left by a departing CMO.

Is It Right for You?

Who Needs a Fractional CMO?

A fractional CMO is the right solution in specific situations. Here are the five scenarios where businesses consistently get the highest return from our fractional CMO service.

A

Series A / B Start-up

You’ve raised your round and need to scale marketing fast, but hiring a full-time CMO at this stage is expensive and risky. A fractional CMO builds the marketing function, hires the initial team, and drives growth until you’re ready to bring a permanent CMO in-house — typically at Series C or beyond.

PE

PE-Backed SME

Private equity investors expect 100-day plans, board-ready reporting, and a marketing strategy that supports the investment thesis. Our fractional CMOs have deep experience in PE-backed environments — they speak the language of returns, EBITDA, and value creation, not just marketing metrics.

FL

Founder-Led Business Scaling

Founder-led marketing works brilliantly until it doesn’t. When the founder’s time is better spent on product, operations, or fundraising — but handing marketing over to a junior team feels risky — a fractional CMO provides the senior layer of strategic oversight that bridges the gap.

M&A

Post-Merger Integration

After an acquisition or merger, marketing strategy and branding must be rapidly rationalised. A fractional CMO with M&A experience leads the post-merger marketing integration — aligning teams, consolidating brand architecture, and establishing a unified go-to-market strategy for the combined business.

PL

CMO on Parental Leave

When your full-time CMO goes on parental leave, a fractional CMO provides continuity of strategic leadership without the risk of appointing an interim who doesn’t understand your business. We provide a seamless handover, maintain momentum, and ensure your marketing team has the senior leadership it needs during the cover period.

The First 90 Days

How Your First 90 Days Look

Every Bambino fractional CMO engagement follows a proven 90-day onboarding framework — structured to deliver a clear, board-ready marketing strategy and begin execution as quickly as possible.

Days 1–30
Audit Phase

Deep-Dive Audit

Your fractional CMO conducts a full marketing audit: reviewing existing strategy (if any), analysing all channel performance data, mapping the customer journey, interviewing key stakeholders, assessing the team and agency setup, and benchmarking against your primary competitors. The output is a prioritised diagnosis of your marketing strengths, weaknesses, and the biggest opportunities for growth.

Days 31–60
Strategy Phase

Strategy & Planning

Based on the audit findings, your fractional CMO builds the full marketing strategy: ICP and positioning, channel mix and budget allocation, 12-month roadmap, OKR framework, team structure recommendations, and agency briefings. This is presented to the board in a structured, investment-grade format. At the end of Day 60, you have a clear, evidence-based marketing strategy ready for execution.

Days 61–90
Execution Phase

Execution & Quick Wins

Strategy is only valuable when it translates into action. In Days 61–90, your fractional CMO begins executing the highest-priority initiatives: briefing agencies, launching or optimising priority channels, implementing the reporting framework, and beginning the team and OKR cadence. The 90-day review then sets the priorities for the next quarter — establishing the ongoing rhythm of your fractional CMO engagement.
