Affiliate Revenue That Doesn’t Cannibalise Your Other Channels

Most affiliate programmes underperform not because the channel doesn’t work, but because they’re poorly structured. Voucher-code sites eating margin on customers who were going to buy anyway. Dormant publishers inflating sign-up numbers. Commission structures that attract the wrong traffic.

Bambino builds affiliate programmes engineered for incremental growth. We select the right network for your sector, recruit high-quality publishers — content creators, comparison sites, loyalty platforms, influencers — and build commission structures that reward genuine new-customer acquisition rather than last-click value.

Common Questions

Affiliate Marketing FAQs

Affiliate marketing is a performance-based channel where publishers (bloggers, review sites, cashback platforms, influencers) promote your products or services in exchange for a commission on sales or leads they generate. You only pay when a result is delivered, making it one of the most cost-efficient acquisition channels for UK businesses.

We work with all major UK affiliate networks including Awin, Rakuten Advertising, Commission Junction (CJ), Impact, and Tradedoubler. We recommend the best network for your sector and manage the full relationship on your behalf, including tracking setup, publisher recruitment, and commission management.

Most programmes start generating meaningful traffic and sales within 60–90 days. Building a mature network of 40+ active publishers typically takes 4–6 months. We fast-track this by proactively recruiting high-quality publishers rather than waiting for them to apply organically.

Incrementality is our biggest focus. We audit publisher overlap with your existing channels, suppress voucher-code and last-click-only publishers that cannibalise existing customers, and use cross-channel attribution to measure true incremental lift. Our clients average 22% genuinely incremental revenue from the affiliate channel.

UK ecommerce programmes typically pay 4–12% on product sales depending on category. Lead-generation programmes pay a fixed CPA of £5–£50 per qualified lead. We model the right commission structure for your margins and competitive landscape before launch to ensure publisher motivation without eroding profitability.

Yes — brand compliance and fraud prevention are non-negotiable. We actively monitor for trademark bidding, coupon leakage, cookie stuffing, and off-brand content. Publishers violating terms are removed immediately. We provide monthly brand safety reports so you always know exactly where your brand is being promoted.

We can do both. If you already have a programme on Awin or another network, we’ll audit its current performance, renegotiate publisher terms, cull non-performing affiliates, and inject new recruitment to reignite growth. Starting fresh is equally straightforward — we handle network selection, technical integration, and publisher outreach from day one.
