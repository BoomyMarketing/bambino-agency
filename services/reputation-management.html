What We Do

What Is Online Reputation Management?

Online reputation management (ORM) is the practice of monitoring, influencing, and improving how your brand appears across search engine results pages (SERPs), review platforms, social media, and the broader web. It combines SERP control — ensuring that when someone searches your brand name, they see content that positively represents you — with review management, brand monitoring, and crisis response.

For UK businesses, ORM is not an optional extra. A single negative article ranking on page one of Google, or a Trustpilot score below 4.0, can meaningfully suppress inbound enquiries and revenue. Bambino’s ORM service takes a data-led, proactive approach to reputation — building the positive content, generating authentic reviews, and monitoring for threats before they escalate into crises.

Whether you need to recover from reputational damage or simply build a stronger foundation before problems arise, our ORM programmes are tailored to your sector, audience, and the platforms that matter most to your customers.

Why Your Online Reputation Matters More Than Ever

UK consumers are more review-conscious than almost any other market. Your online reputation is the single most powerful — and often most neglected — conversion lever your business has.

88% of UK consumers check online reviews before making a purchase decision. For high-consideration purchases — legal, medical, financial, property — that figure is even higher.

Research shows that a one-star drop in a business’s average review score correlates with a 5–9% reduction in revenue. A proactive ORM strategy protects that revenue baseline.

94% of consumers say a negative online review has convinced them to avoid a business. A proactively managed reputation prevents negative content from silently costing you customers.

Minimum Star Rating to Convert

The majority of UK consumers require a minimum 4.0-star rating before they will consider engaging with a business. Businesses below this threshold see significantly reduced enquiry rates.

Our ORM Services

What Our ORM Agency Delivers

A complete online reputation management service covering every layer of your brand’s digital presence — from SERP control and review generation to real-time monitoring and crisis response.

SERP Management

Review Generation

Brand Monitoring

We deploy near real-time monitoring across search engines, social media, forums, news publications, and review platforms to alert you the moment your brand is mentioned. Early detection of potential issues allows a proactive response before negative content gains traction or ranks.
