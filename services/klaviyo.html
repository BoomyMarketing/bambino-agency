The 12 Essential Flows

The 12 Must-Have Klaviyo Flows

Most UK ecommerce stores have 2–3 flows active. The difference between a Klaviyo account generating 15% of revenue and one generating 40% is almost always the breadth and quality of the flow library. We build all 12.

01

Welcome Series

Your highest-converting flow. A 3–5 email sequence that delivers your brand story, bestsellers, social proof, and a first-purchase incentive. We A/B test subject lines, send times, and discount thresholds.

02

Abandoned Cart

The single biggest revenue recovery opportunity. A multi-step sequence targeting customers who added to cart but didn’t complete purchase. Typically recovers 8–15% of abandoned cart revenue.

03

Browse Abandonment

Triggered when a subscriber views a product page but doesn’t add to cart. Catches intent earlier in the funnel and often outperforms abandoned cart for high-consideration categories.

04

Post-Purchase

Drive repeat purchases, cross-sells, and LTV. Sequences include order confirmation content, product education, cross-sell recommendations, and review requests — timed to match your delivery window.

05

Win-Back

Reactivate lapsed customers who haven’t purchased in 90, 120, or 180 days (depending on your purchase frequency). Personalised with their last purchase category and targeted incentives.

06

Price Drop

Automatically notifies subscribers who have browsed or wishlist-ed a product when its price drops. A high-converting, zero-effort flow that closes purchase intent that was previously price-sensitive.

07

Back in Stock

Captures subscribers on out-of-stock product pages and notifies them the moment inventory is restored. Converts at 20–35% for high-demand products — with no paid media required.

08

VIP

Identifies your highest-value customers and delivers exclusive early access, priority service, and loyalty rewards. Increases repeat purchase rates and average order value for your top 10% of buyers.

09

Sunset

Cleans your list by identifying chronically unengaged subscribers, sending a re-engagement sequence, and suppressing those who don’t respond. Protects your sender reputation and reduces Klaviyo costs.

10

Review Request

Timed to your average delivery window, this flow prompts post-purchase reviews on your platform of choice (Google, Trustpilot, Shopify, etc.) — driving social proof that improves conversion site-wide.

11

Birthday

Personalised birthday emails with an exclusive offer sent 7 days before and on a subscriber’s birthday. One of the highest open-rate flows in any Klaviyo account — with near-zero unsubscribe rates.

12

Replenishment

For consumable products. Timed to re-order windows based on product type and purchase history, this flow nudges customers to repurchase before they run out — dramatically reducing churn for subscription-prone categories.

Segmentation

Segmentation Strategy

The right message to the right person at the right time. Our segmentation approach uses purchase behaviour, engagement signals, and predictive analytics to ensure every send is relevant — protecting deliverability and maximising revenue per recipient.

RFM Segmentation

Recency, Frequency, Monetary segmentation divides your list into behavioural tiers based on how recently, how often, and how much each customer has spent. RFM segments power your VIP flows, win-back flows, and campaign send strategy — ensuring your best customers are rewarded and your at-risk customers are reactivated before they churn permanently.

Predictive Analytics

Klaviyo’s predictive analytics layer forecasts each contact’s next order date, expected lifetime value, and churn risk based on their historical behaviour. We use these predictions to target customers with replenishment flows before they run out, win-back campaigns before they leave, and upsell emails timed to their predicted next purchase window.

Behaviour-Based Segments

Beyond RFM, we build segments based on product category affinity, channel preference (email vs SMS), discount sensitivity, browse depth, and engagement level. These segments power both automated flows and campaign targeting — ensuring a customer who only buys from the skincare category never receives irrelevant homeware promotions.

Compliance

UK GDPR & PECR Compliance in Klaviyo

Every Klaviyo account we manage is configured to meet UK GDPR and PECR requirements. For UK ecommerce brands, this means separate opt-ins for email and SMS, consent timestamps stored on every contact record, and automated sunset flows that delete non-consenting contacts within your defined retention period.

Under PECR, you must have explicit consent to send marketing emails and SMS to UK consumers. We configure Klaviyo’s opt-in forms with separate checkboxes for each channel, double opt-in where required, and clear consent language that meets ICO standards. Every form captures and stores consent source, timestamp, and the exact consent text shown.

Our list cleaning and sunset flows ensure that contacts who have not engaged within your retention window are automatically re-consented or suppressed — keeping your list healthy, your sender reputation strong, and your ICO compliance maintained without manual effort.
