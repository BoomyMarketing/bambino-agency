How It Works

Our 4-Step Process

A clear, proven methodology — from discovery to measurable results.

1

Discovery & Call Mapping

We analyse your current inbound call patterns, map the ideal call flow, define qualification criteria, and design the conversational logic for your voice AI.

2

Configuration & Integration

We configure the voice AI with your branding, UK English voice settings, and business-specific knowledge — then integrate it with your CRM, calendar, and lead management systems.

3

Testing & Launch

Extensive testing across diverse call scenarios before going live. We test edge cases, unusual requests, and escalation pathways to ensure the system handles real-world calls confidently.

4

Monitor & Optimise

We review call recordings and transcripts weekly, identifying gaps in the AI's knowledge or logic and continuously refining responses for better qualification and lead capture rates.
