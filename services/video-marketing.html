What We Do

What Is Video Marketing?

Video marketing is the strategic use of video content to attract, engage, and convert your target audience across multiple digital channels. It goes far beyond simply producing a brand video and hoping people watch it. Effective video marketing requires a content strategy aligned with your buyer journey, production that reflects your brand standards, distribution that reaches your audience where they actually spend time, and analytics that prove commercial impact.

For UK businesses, video marketing represents one of the highest-ROI content investments available. The Cisco Visual Networking Index projects that 88% of all online consumer internet traffic will be video by 2026. Brands that have already built a video marketing capability are compounding that advantage month by month — building YouTube audiences, dominating LinkedIn feeds, and generating organic reach on Instagram and TikTok that paid advertising struggles to replicate.

Bambino’s video marketing service covers the full lifecycle: strategy, scripting, production management, editing, distribution, video SEO, and monthly performance reporting. We work with B2B and B2C brands across all sectors, producing everything from polished brand films to high-volume short-form social content.

Content Types

Video Content Types We Produce

From high-production brand films to quick-turn social shorts, we produce video content designed for the platform it will live on and the audience it needs to reach.

Brand Films

High-production brand films that tell your story, communicate your values, and make an emotional connection with your audience. Brand films anchor your website homepage, feature in paid campaigns, and serve as the flagship content for your LinkedIn and YouTube channels. Typically 60–180 seconds in length, designed for maximum impact.

Explainer Videos

Animated or live-action explainer videos that make complex products, services, or processes immediately understandable. Explainers are proven to increase landing page conversion rates, reduce customer service queries, and shorten B2B sales cycles by giving prospects the information they need in the format they prefer to consume it.

Product Demos

Video demonstrations that show your product or service in action, addressing the objections that hold potential buyers back. Product demo videos are particularly effective for SaaS, ecommerce, and professional services, where understanding how something works is a key part of the buying decision. We script, produce, and optimise demos for both web and sales enablement use.

Customer Testimonials

Authentic, on-camera testimonials from your real customers — the most persuasive content type at the consideration and decision stages of the buyer journey. We handle everything from customer briefing and question preparation through to filming, editing, and producing polished testimonial assets for website, paid ads, and social media.

Social Shorts

High-volume, platform-native short-form video content for Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and LinkedIn. Social shorts are designed to generate organic reach, build brand awareness, and drive profile follows at the top of the funnel. We produce native vertical (9:16) content specifically optimised for each platform’s algorithm and audience behaviour.

Webinars & Livestreams

Planned, promoted, and produced webinars and livestream sessions that build authority, generate leads, and create a library of repurposable long-form content. We handle technical setup, co-host production, and post-event editing — turning a single live session into clips, highlights, and written content that continues to generate value for months.

Distribution

Where We Distribute Your Video Content

Great video content only generates ROI when it reaches the right audience on the right platform. We manage distribution across every channel where your target audience actually spends time.

YouTube

The world’s second-largest search engine. We optimise titles, descriptions, tags, chapters, cards, and end screens for maximum organic reach. YouTube SEO means your content keeps generating views and leads months after publication — compounding your investment over time.

LinkedIn

The highest-quality B2B audience in the UK. LinkedIn native video consistently outperforms links and image posts in the feed algorithm. We produce LinkedIn-specific video content — thought leadership clips, case study teasers, and company news — formatted for autoplay and optimised for professional audiences.

Instagram Reels

Instagram’s primary reach mechanism for new audience discovery. We produce native 9:16 Reels content with captions, hooks designed for the first two seconds, and formats that match current algorithm preferences to maximise organic reach to UK audiences.

TikTok

The fastest-growing platform for organic reach in the UK, with 23 million monthly active users. TikTok’s algorithm rewards authenticity and entertainment value above production quality — making it accessible to businesses of all sizes when content is planned and produced by people who understand the platform.

Website

On-site video increases time on page, reduces bounce rate, and directly improves conversion rates on landing pages and product pages. We embed video with performance in mind — using schema markup for video SEO, lazy loading for Core Web Vitals, and captions for accessibility and search engine indexing.

Email

Video in email increases click-through rates by up to 300%. We produce video content specifically formatted for email marketing campaigns — animated GIF previews that link to full videos, video thumbnails with play buttons, and personalised video sequences for nurture and sales engagement programmes.
