What We Do

What Is Lifecycle Marketing?

Lifecycle marketing is the practice of tailoring your marketing messages, channels, and timing to the specific stage a customer occupies in their relationship with your brand. Rather than sending the same message to everyone, lifecycle marketing maps the journey from Awareness through Consideration, Purchase, Retention, and Advocacy — and delivers precisely calibrated communications at each stage.

A new subscriber who has never purchased needs a different message from a customer who bought once six months ago and has not returned. A trial user who has not yet activated needs different nudges from a power user approaching renewal. Lifecycle marketing uses behavioural data and automation to make every interaction feel relevant and timely — at scale. The result is a compounding improvement in conversion rates, LTV, and churn across the entire customer base.

The Customer Journey

The Five Customer Lifecycle Stages

Every customer passes through five stages. We build programmes that move them efficiently through each one — and keep them there.

01

Attract

Lead magnet sequences, welcome series, paid retargeting audiences, and SEO content to convert strangers into engaged prospects.

02

Engage & Convert

Nurture sequences, product education emails, social proof campaigns, and abandoned browse/cart flows to turn prospects into first-time buyers.

03

Onboard

Post-purchase sequences, onboarding emails, setup guides, and check-in messages that ensure new customers reach their first value moment quickly.

04

Retain & Grow

Replenishment reminders, cross-sell and upsell flows, loyalty programme milestones, and win-back campaigns for customers showing churn signals.

05

Advocate

Referral programme invitations, VIP community access, review and testimonial requests, and ambassador campaigns for your highest-LTV customers.

Common Questions

Lifecycle Marketing FAQs

Lifecycle marketing is the practice of tailoring your marketing messages, channels, and timing to the specific stage a customer or prospect occupies in their relationship with your brand. Rather than sending the same message to everyone, lifecycle marketing delivers relevant communications at the moment they are most likely to influence behaviour — from a welcome email for a new subscriber to a win-back campaign for a lapsed customer. The goal is to move every individual through Awareness, Consideration, Purchase, Retention, and Advocacy as efficiently as possible.

Email marketing is one channel; lifecycle marketing is a strategy that spans multiple channels — email, SMS, push notifications, in-app messages, paid retargeting, and community. Lifecycle marketing uses customer data to determine which stage someone is in and then selects the right message, the right channel, and the right moment. Email is often the primary workhorse of lifecycle marketing, but a lifecycle programme also uses SMS for time-sensitive messages, retargeting for re-engagement, and loyalty programmes for long-term advocacy.

At a minimum, you need an email list with basic segmentation (new vs existing customers), a record of purchase history, and some form of behavioural data (pages visited, products viewed, emails opened). Most businesses already have this in their CRM, ESP, or ecommerce platform. If your data is fragmented across multiple tools, we begin with a data audit and integration phase to consolidate it into a single source of truth before building lifecycle flows. You do not need a perfect data set to start — we work with what you have and improve it over time.
