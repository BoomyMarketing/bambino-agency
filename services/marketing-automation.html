What Is Marketing Automation?

Software That Works So Your Team Doesn’t Have To

Marketing automation is software that automates repetitive marketing tasks — sending email sequences, scoring leads based on behaviour, updating CRM records, segmenting contacts, and triggering personalised messages at exactly the right moment. Rather than manually following up with every enquiry or sending the same onboarding email for the hundredth time, automation handles these processes in the background 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

The result is faster lead response times, more consistent nurture sequences, better sales intelligence (because every interaction is tracked), and a significant reduction in the manual administrative burden on your marketing and sales teams. UK businesses using marketing automation report an average 14.5% increase in sales productivity (Nucleus Research) — not because they hire more people, but because their existing team focuses on high-value work rather than repetitive tasks.

From a simple lead nurture sequence through to a complex multi-branch workflow spanning email, SMS, CRM, and sales alerts, we design and build automation that fits your business — not generic templates lifted from a platform playbook.

What We Automate

8 Marketing Tasks We Automate for UK Businesses

Every hour your team spends on these tasks manually is an hour not spent on strategy, relationships, and growth. Let automation handle the repetitive work.

Lead Nurture Sequences

Multi-step email sequences that educate, build trust, and move prospects through your funnel automatically after each enquiry or download.

Lead Scoring

Automatically score leads based on behaviour (page views, email opens, downloads, pricing page visits) so your sales team always knows who is ready to buy.

CRM Data Enrichment

Automatically append firmographic and contact data to new CRM records using tools like Clearbit, so your sales team always starts with full context.

Abandoned Cart & Browse

Trigger sequences for eCommerce shoppers who abandon their cart or browse high-value product pages without purchasing — recovering lost revenue automatically.

Re-Engagement Campaigns

Automatically identify and re-engage contacts who have gone cold — before they churn or unsubscribe — with targeted win-back sequences.

Onboarding Sequences

Welcome new customers, trial users, or subscribers with a structured onboarding sequence that drives product adoption and reduces early churn.

Review Requests

Automatically request reviews from satisfied customers at the optimal moment post-purchase or post-project — improving your Google, Trustpilot, and industry platform ratings.

Internal Sales Alerts

Notify your sales team automatically when a lead hits a score threshold, visits a key page, or takes a trigger action — so they follow up at exactly the right moment.

Our 5-Step Marketing Automation Process

From audit to live automation in as little as 4 weeks — with the highest-impact workflows deployed first.

Salesforce Marketing Cloud suits enterprise businesses with complex requirements. We will recommend the right platform for your specific needs, tech stack, and budget during your free audit.

Email marketing involves sending broadcast emails to lists of contacts. Marketing automation uses behaviour-triggered logic to send the right email to the right person at the right time — based on what they have clicked, downloaded, visited, or purchased. Automation also extends beyond email to include CRM updates, lead scoring, internal sales notifications, SMS, and personalised website content. Think of email marketing as one channel; marketing automation is the system that orchestrates all channels intelligently.

More complex implementations involving CRM migration, multi-platform integrations, or custom lead scoring models can take 8–12 weeks. We phase delivery so that high-impact workflows go live first — you’ll see automation working within the first month regardless of overall programme complexity.
