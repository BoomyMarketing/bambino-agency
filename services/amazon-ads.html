How It Works

Our 4-Step Amazon Ads Process

From account audit to sustainable ACoS reduction — our proven Amazon advertising methodology for UK sellers.

1

Account Audit

We audit your Seller or Vendor Central account, reviewing campaign structure, match type strategy, bidding, negative keyword management, and listing quality — identifying the specific issues driving high ACoS and wasted spend.

2

Keyword Research

We conduct deep keyword research using Amazon’s search term reports, third-party tools, and competitor analysis to build comprehensive keyword lists segmented by intent, match type, and margin priority.

3

Campaign Build

We rebuild campaign architecture with proper product segmentation, match type isolation, and bid strategies aligned to your target ACoS. Sponsored Brands and Display are layered in based on budget and brand objectives.

4

Optimise & Scale

We run weekly search term harvesting, moving converting search terms to exact match campaigns and adding negatives to prevent waste. Monthly reports show ACoS, ROAS, and market share trends with clear commentary on what changed and why.

Common Questions

Amazon Ads FAQs

We manage all Amazon advertising types: Sponsored Products (appear in search results and product pages), Sponsored Brands (banner ads featuring your brand logo and multiple products), Sponsored Display (retargeting on and off Amazon), and Amazon DSP for programmatic display where applicable. We select the right mix based on your goals, budget, and product catalogue.

ACoS (Advertising Cost of Sale) is the percentage of revenue spent on advertising — calculated as ad spend divided by ad revenue. A 20% ACoS means you spent £20 to generate £100 in sales. ROAS (Return on Ad Spend) is the inverse — revenue divided by spend. A 20% ACoS equals a 5x ROAS. We use both metrics depending on your preference and reporting requirements.

We recommend a minimum of £500–£1,000/month in Amazon ad spend to generate sufficient data for meaningful optimisation. This varies significantly by category — competitive categories like supplements and electronics require higher budgets. Our management starts from £800/mo. We’ll recommend a specific budget after reviewing your products, category, and target ACoS during a free audit.

Yes — listing optimisation is integral to Amazon Ads performance. A well-optimised listing converts at a higher rate, which improves your ACoS and quality score in Amazon’s A9 algorithm. We review and optimise product titles, bullet points, descriptions, backend keywords, and A+ Content as part of our Amazon management service.

Yes — many UK ecommerce businesses sell on both their own website and Amazon. We manage both channels with a unified strategy, preventing cannibalisation and ensuring your total advertising investment is allocated efficiently between channels based on ROAS and margin. We report on both platforms in a single monthly dashboard.

Initial campaign restructuring typically produces visible ACoS improvements within 4–6 weeks. More significant gains from keyword harvesting and bid optimisation typically emerge within 60–90 days. Our clients see an average 30% ACoS reduction within 90 days of account restructure. Amazon’s algorithm rewards consistent advertising history, so early investment pays dividends over time.

Yes — we work with both Fulfilment by Amazon (FBA) and Fulfilled by Merchant (FBM) UK sellers, as well as Amazon Vendor Central accounts. Our approach is tailored to your fulfilment model and account type. FBA sellers typically benefit more from Sponsored Brands and Display, while FBM sellers often see faster results from well-structured Sponsored Products campaigns.
