1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, to improve user experience, and to provide information to website operators.

Cookies are not harmful to your device and cannot run programmes or deliver viruses. They allow websites to remember you and your preferences, so you do not need to re-enter information every time you visit.

In addition to cookies, we may use similar tracking technologies such as pixel tags, web beacons, and local storage. This Cookie Policy covers all such technologies collectively.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies on bambinoagency.com for the following purposes:

  • To ensure the Website functions correctly and securely
  • To remember your cookie preferences
  • To analyse how visitors use our Website so we can improve it
  • To measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns
  • To deliver relevant advertisements on third-party platforms
  • To provide certain enhanced functionality such as live chat or embedded content

We comply with the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations 2003 (PECR) and UK GDPR when using cookies. Where we rely on your consent for non-essential cookies, we will always ask for it before placing those cookies on your device.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookies can be categorised in several ways. By duration:

  • Session cookies are temporary and are deleted when you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies remain on your device for a defined period or until you delete them.

By source:

  • First-party cookies are set by us directly.
  • Third-party cookies are set by external services we use (e.g. Google Analytics, Meta Pixel).

We recommend that you review the privacy and cookie policies of any third-party services you interact with. Key third parties whose technologies we may use include:

9. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

9.1 Our Cookie Consent Tool

When you first visit our Website, you will be presented with a cookie consent banner that allows you to accept or decline non-essential cookies. You can update your preferences at any time by clicking the “Cookie Settings” link in the footer of our Website.

9.2 Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View the cookies that have been set
  • Delete all cookies or cookies from specific websites
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block all cookies (note that this may affect the functionality of many websites)

For information on how to manage cookies in your browser, visit:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Microsoft Edge
  • Apple Safari

9.3 Opt-Out Links

You can also opt out of specific third-party tracking using these direct links:

  • Google Analytics Opt-out
  • Meta Ad Preferences
  • Your Online Choices (UK) — opt out of interest-based advertising from multiple networks

In accordance with PECR and UK GDPR, we only place non-essential cookies on your device after obtaining your clear, informed consent. You may withdraw or amend your consent at any time using our cookie consent tool or your browser settings. Withdrawing consent will not affect the lawfulness of any processing based on consent before you withdrew it.

If you are accessing our Website from a jurisdiction where different consent rules apply, we will comply with the requirements of the applicable local law.
