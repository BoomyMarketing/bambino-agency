Why Industry Matters

One Size Never Fits All

The way someone searches for a solicitor is nothing like how they search for running shoes. A dental patient comparing implant costs behaves differently from a CFO evaluating SaaS platforms. Search intent, competitive dynamics, seasonal patterns, regulatory constraints, and conversion paths vary dramatically by sector — and your digital marketing strategy should reflect every one of those differences.

Bambino's team includes specialists who have worked exclusively within ecommerce, legal, property, healthcare, finance, SaaS, and hospitality. We know which keywords convert in your sector, which ad formats your buyers respond to, and how to build authority in your competitive landscape — without starting from scratch every time.
