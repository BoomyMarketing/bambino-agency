You’re competing with Amazon, established brand.com sites, and thousands of resellers — all fighting for the same search terms, the same Google Shopping impressions, and the same limited buyer attention. Common Pain Points

Ecommerce Challenges We Solve

Every ecommerce business hits the same walls. Here’s how Bambino breaks through them.

Competing with Amazon & Brand.com

When Amazon and the manufacturer themselves are ranking above you, you need a different strategy — not a bigger budget. We identify the keyword niches, content angles, and SERP features where you can win without going head-to-head with billion-pound marketing budgets. Long-tail category SEO, expert content, and review-led product pages are your competitive edge.

Seasonal Traffic Spikes

Black Friday, Christmas, Valentine’s Day, summer sales — ecommerce success is built on preparation, not reaction. We build your seasonal strategy months in advance: pre-loading stock-aware landing pages, ramping ad budgets ahead of demand curves, and creating gift-guide content that starts ranking before your competitors have even briefed their agencies.

High Ad Spend with Poor ROAS

Many ecommerce brands scale spend without scaling efficiency. Poor product feed quality, mis-matched bidding strategies, and untargeted audience segments all bleed budget. Our Shopping specialists audit your feed structure, apply product-level ROAS targets based on margin data, and segment campaigns by product category performance — stopping wasteful spend on low-margin SKUs.

Thin Content on Product Pages Killing SEO

Ecommerce sites with thousands of near-identical product pages — manufacturer descriptions, generic titles, no unique content — are penalised by Google for thin content and keyword cannibalisation. We implement content templates that scale across your catalogue, add unique value to each page, and create category-level authority content that lifts rankings sitewide.
